Purpose & Context

Steve is a Professor at SUNY Polytechnic Institute and Co-Director of the AIX Center. Core research agenda: Inference Cycle Analysis (ICA) — capturing, analyzing, and tracing the provenance of human-AI interactions as discrete analytical units across temporal, user-based, topical, and organizational dimensions.

Flagship project: DAC-AC (Development of Analytical Capabilities for Information Campaign Assessment), DoD/Army contract with DEVCOM Analysis Center. Deliverables: 1.1 case studies & experimental results, 1.2 analytical tools, 1.3 publications & analyses.

Central methodological anchor: M-DUN framework (Modeling Dense Urban Networks), developed by Perceptronics Solutions. 12-dimension societal vulnerability assessment with entropy-based measurement and radar chart visualization. ICA is the analytical provenance layer M-DUN lacks — complementary, not competitive.
