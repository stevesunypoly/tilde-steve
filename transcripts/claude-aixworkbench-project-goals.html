## Slide 3: AIXworkbench: Built on Four Principles

An **open-source workspace** built on OpenWebUI that runs on ordinary laptops in familiar browsers. Use AI without the internet or through APIs.

### 🔒 Security Your data stays in your environment, not someone else's cloud

### ⚖️ Responsibility You control how AI is used, not a corporate algorithm

### 🔄 Replicability Your workflows can be reproduced and verified by others

### 🤝 Shareability Pass techniques to your team, students, and community

---

## Slide 4: Feature 1 - Knowledge Bases

### Build Your Research Library

- Upload PDFs, documents, and research papers - Connect to external document repositories - Organize materials by course, project, or topic - Keep institutional knowledge secure and accessible

**Why it matters:** Build custom AI assistants trained on your curriculum, research corpus, or institutional documents.

---

## Slide 5: Feature 2 - Prompting Knowledge Bases

### Query Your Documents

- Ask questions directly to your uploaded materials - Get citations and references to source documents - Compare answers across multiple documents - Create study guides from course readings

**Why it matters:** Students and researchers can interact with primary sources, generate literature reviews, and verify information against trusted materials.

---

## Slide 6: Feature 3 - Multiple Model Selection

### Compare AI Models Side-by-Side

- Access local models (Ollama) and API models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) - Send the same prompt to multiple models simultaneously - Compare response quality, style, and accuracy - Choose the best model for specific tasks

**Why it matters:** Teach students critical AI literacy by comparing models. Conduct research on model performance and bias.

---

## Slide 7: Feature 4 - Shared Reusable Prompts

### Build and Share Prompt Templates

- Create standardized prompts for common tasks - Share prompts with your team, class, or department - Access community-contributed prompt libraries - Ensure consistency across research or teaching

**Why it matters:** _This is shareability in action._ Students can use proven prompts. Researchers can replicate experiments. Institutions can standardize AI use.

---

## Slide 8: Feature 5 - Model Parameters

### Control Inference Settings

- Adjust temperature for creativity vs. precision - Modify token limits for response length - Set top-p, top-k, and other advanced parameters - Document parameter settings for reproducibility

**Why it matters:** _This is replicability in action._ Record exact conditions of AI interactions. Enable peer review of AI-assisted research. Teach students how models actually work.

---

## Slide 9: Two Deployment Options

### 💻 Personal AIXworkbench **For individual faculty and researchers**

- Runs on your laptop or desktop - Works offline with local models - Connect to APIs when needed - Complete control over your data - Perfect for personal research and course prep

### 👥 Team AIXworkbench **For classrooms, labs, and small organizations**

- Serves 2-25 users via web app - Shared knowledge bases and prompts - Centralized model access - User management and permissions - Ideal for courses, research groups, departments
